# Privacy Policy for MANO Attendance App

**Last Updated:** December 8, 2024

## Introduction

MANO ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MANO Attendance mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you use our App, we may collect the following personal information:
- **User Credentials:** Username and password for authentication
- **Name:** Your full name for identification purposes
- **Authentication Tokens:** JWT tokens for secure session management

### 2. Location Data
- **GPS Coordinates:** We collect your precise location (latitude and longitude) when you check in or check out
- **Location Address:** The address corresponding to your GPS coordinates is provided by our backend server
- **Purpose:** Location data is used solely to verify your attendance location and is required for the core functionality of the App

### 3. Camera and Photos
- **Photos:** We capture photos using your device camera during check-in and check-out
- **Purpose:** Photos are used for attendance verification and identity confirmation
- **Storage:** Photos are uploaded to our secure servers and may be stored for record-keeping purposes

### 4. Device Information
- **Device Type:** Information about your mobile device (Android version, device model)
- **App Version:** The version of the MANO app you are using

### 5. Usage Data
- **Attendance Records:** Check-in and check-out timestamps
- **Session Data:** Information about your attendance sessions
- **Cache Data:** Temporary storage of attendance records on your device for offline access

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Attendance Tracking:** To record and manage your attendance check-ins and check-outs
2. **Identity Verification:** To verify your identity through photos and location data
3. **Service Provision:** To provide, maintain, and improve the App's functionality
4. **Authentication:** To authenticate your identity and manage your sessions
5. **Record Keeping:** To maintain accurate attendance records for your organization
6. **Analytics:** To understand how users interact with the App and improve user experience

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage
- **Server Storage:** Your attendance data, photos, and location information are stored on our secure servers (erp.mano.co.in)
- **Local Cache:** The App temporarily caches attendance records on your device for faster access and offline viewing
- **Session Storage:** Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:
- **Encryption:** Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
- **Authentication:** Secure JWT token-based authentication
- **Access Control:** Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
- **Secure Storage:** Photos and data are stored on secure servers with appropriate safeguards

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

1. **With Your Employer:** Attendance data, photos, and location information are shared with your organization/employer for attendance management purposes
2. **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests
3. **Service Providers:** We may share data with trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our App (e.g., cloud hosting providers), under strict confidentiality agreements

## Permissions Required

The App requires the following permissions to function:

1. **Camera Permission:** To capture photos during check-in and check-out
2. **Location Permission:** To record your GPS coordinates for attendance verification
3. **Storage Permission:** To temporarily store photos and cache attendance data
4. **Internet Permission:** To communicate with our servers and upload attendance data

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings, but this may limit the App's functionality.

## Data Retention

- **Attendance Records:** We retain your attendance records as long as required by your organization's policies or legal requirements
- **Photos:** Photos are retained for the duration specified by your organization
- **Cache Data:** Local cache data is cleared when you log out or uninstall the App
- **Authentication Tokens:** Tokens are deleted when you log out

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

1. **Access:** Request access to your personal data
2. **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
3. **Deletion:** Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal and organizational requirements)
4. **Data Portability:** Request a copy of your data in a structured format
5. **Withdrawal of Consent:** Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact your organization's administrator or reach out to us at the contact information below.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.
